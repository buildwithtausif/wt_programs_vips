HTML Headings (H1-H6)

Purpose
Heading tags are used to define headings or titles in a document. <h1> is the most important (largest) heading, and <h6> is the least important (smallest).
Hierarchy
You should use headings in order to create a logical document structure. Search engines use this structure to understand your content.
Example
<h1>Main Title</h1>
<h2>Subtitle</h2>
<h3>Sub-section</h3>
<h4>Sub-section</h4>
<h5>Sub-section</h5>
<h6>Sub-section</h6>
